    About Us

    Our mission is to connect you to artisans, vendors, and makers who tell a story with their craft – a story of skill, passion, purpose, history, and culture – and:


    Discover goods that reflect where you are and what you value

    Learn craft by immersing yourself in experiences with skilled craftspeople

    Joining communities of fellow makers and doers who are stringing to make the world a more beautiful and thoughtful place


    We want to help you invest in artists, goods, experiences, and communities who are doing good with their craft.
